We invite you to a Journey of Innovation and Design

Let's honor Imagination, Growth, and Community

Join us for a special evening filled with inspiration, exploration, and collaboration! This showcase will highlight the remarkable projects of our IDP3 and UED students, along with special Spotlight presentations on the Royal Canadian Air Force Association (Local 427 Wing) initiative. Discover the creativity, dedication, and vision behind each portfolio as students share their work across diverse fields.
This is more than a celebration of talent — it's an opportunity to connect, exchange ideas, and inspire the next generation of professionals. Your presence, encouragement, and feedback will play a vital role in supporting their growth and future success.

Video Player

Project Detail

Level 4 students collaborated on a project for the Royal Canadian Air Force Association (Local 427 Wing), creating a digital initiative to preserve the legacy of the last remaining RCAF building in London, ON, while engaging the community and honoring Canadian veterans.

Discover the innovative projects of our IDP3 and UED students, including Spotlight presentations that highlight the legacy and community impact of the Royal Canadian Air Force Association (Local 427 Wing).

Experience the enthusiasm and talent of our students as they present their portfolios, demonstrating their expertise in interactive media, UX design, web development, and more.
